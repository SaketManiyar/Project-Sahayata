About

Wouldn’t it be satisfying to do something related to COVID-19 as it has rapidly affected our day-to-day life, from disrupting the world trade to plunging the world into distress after the sudden hike in people getting infected by the disease.

Face masks are becoming the new normal. To limit the spread of this infectious disease, mandatory face-mask rules are now becoming common in public settings around the globe and in addition to that many public service providers and government require people to wear face-masks covering both mouth and nose while in public places using public services.

Aim of project

So our project presents a simplified approach to achieve this purpose using some basic Machine Learning packages. Our project detects the face from the image correctly and then identifies if it has a mask on it or not. It is also designed for multiple aspects like :

  • Face Detection
  • Mask Detection
  • Security Measures
  • Temperature Screening (To measure one’s temperature without getting close to them and record the body temperature)

and give all this information to the user through an Android Application. Paired with the face recognition system, it also send an alert message of the person arriving at the door if his/her temperature is over the threshold value…..

Our mission

We “Team Sahayata” do hope that these tough times come to an end and urge you to make Sanitization, Wearing Mask and Maintaining Social Distance your Habit. :)
